We Vote!

Are you currently registered to vote in Michigan?

Please check if you are registered , then return to this app.

If you have a Michigan driver's license or state ID, please apply online for an absent voter ballot. Otherwise please complete an Absent Voter Ballot Application

  1. Enter your information in Section 1
  2. If given the option, please sign up to automatically receive an absent ballot for future elections.
  3. Sign and date
  4. e-mail or mail to your clerk

If you have a Michigan driver's license or state ID, you may register to vote online . Otherwise please complete a Michigan Voter Registration Application

  • If given the option, please sign up to automatically receive an absent ballot for future elections.

You must be a Michigan resident; if you do not yet have a Michigan address, please come back later.

In the past, have you ever been registered to vote in Michigan?

Hand-deliver the completed form to your clerk , OR

Enclose a copy of any one of the following with your application.

  • Driver's license from any state
  • State ID card from any state
  • Current and valid photo identification
  • Paycheck stub
  • Utility bill
  • Bank statement
  • A government document

Please make sure this document lists your name and address.

Mail the completed application to your clerk
